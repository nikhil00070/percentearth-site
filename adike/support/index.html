Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a farm project?
Create from the setup screen after login. Name your farm, set tree count, and get a 4-digit share code to invite your team.
How do I invite team members?
Share your 4-digit project code with workers and family. They enter it in 'Join Project' to see the same farm data in real time.
How do I track arecanut harvests?
Use the Harvest tab to log each harvest with quick entry or tree-wise tracking. Record the date, quantity, and any notes for your records.
How do I manage expenses?
Go to the Expenses tab to log operating costs, fertilizer purchases, and other farm expenses. Categorize entries and generate PDF reports for your records.
How does the marketplace work?
Record sales, manage buyers, and track revenue by channel from the Sales tab. Monitor your earnings over time with detailed transaction history.
What premium features are available?
Premium unlocks advanced insights, detailed analytics, unlimited PDF report generation, and priority support. Check the app for current pricing and plans.
What languages are supported?
English, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam. Change your language from the More tab at any time.
How do I delete my account?
Log out from the More tab. To delete all your data permanently, contact us at hello@percentearth.co and we will process your request within 30 days.
