Earth is our only shareholder

Regenerating sustainable, local, minimalist, organic, and natural ecosystems.

We operate in harmony with nature, embracing the belief that less can be both sufficient and fulfilling. Every percentage point of improvement — in soil health, water conservation, or carbon reduction — moves us closer to a sustainable future.

"Our goal is simple: to regenerate depleted parts of the Earth, one farm, one space, or one ecosystem at a time."

% Earth Collective is not just a single-family farming business — it's a community collective where our methodologies and techniques are shared across farms within our network. Rooted in a deep respect for the planet and a commitment to regenerative and restorative farming, we seek to educate and revolutionize the way we grow and consume food.

Our mission is to restore ecosystems, empower communities, and provide nourishment that benefits both people and the planet. Through sustainable practices that enhance biodiversity, restore soil health, and sequester carbon, we aim to produce high-quality, nutrient-rich food. % Earth is about more than farming — it's about healing the planet.

The % Earth Difference

How We Work

We go beyond organic by implementing practices that not only avoid harm but actively heal the land.

🌱

Regenerative Agriculture

Rebuilding organic soil quality for microorganisms to thrive. Crop rotation, cover cropping, and holistic grazing that actively heals the land.

🔍

Transparency

From farm to table, complete transparency in our operations. Trace your food back to the source and see the positive impact your purchase makes.

🤝

Community Empowerment

Working hand-in-hand with local communities, providing fair wages, educational programs, and opportunities for collaboration.

🌍

Sustainability at Scale

Creating a blueprint for sustainable farming that can be replicated globally. Scaling regenerative practices one farm at a time.

📱

Product Development

Building technology that connects people with food, nature, and farming — apps for dish discovery, plant care, and farm management.

🏡

Farm Stays & Education

Workshops, farm tours, eco homesteads, and educational programs to spread the knowledge of regenerative practices and sustainable living.
